About Us

At Gwarko Restaurant, we believe in the art of culinary creation, meticulously crafting delightful dishes using the freshest local ingredients. Established in 2045 B.S. (1988 A.D.), our family-run kitchen has grown to become a cherished hub for food lovers in search of authentic taste and unparalleled comfort.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to share the rich flavors of Nepali and international cuisine with our community. Over the decades, we've continually refined our recipes, blended traditional techniques with modern culinary innovations, and fostered a warm, inviting atmosphere for all our guests. We are committed to sustainable practices, sourcing ingredients from local farmers and supporting our community.

Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration, Gwarko Restaurant promises an unforgettable dining experience where every dish tells a story of passion and dedication.
